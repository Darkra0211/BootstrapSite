About Page

We here at Planendar are dedicated to helping guide you to future success in your career, whether it be applying for a job, graduate school, or an internship
 We offer a wide range of services in order to help you succeed, these include a calendar to help you plan out your goals, assistance from experts, and resume reviews. If you subscribe to our service, we can also connect you directly with an assistant and help you every step of the way in achieving your future goals, whatever it may be. If you wish to have a more directed experience our subscription service for only 5.99 a month can assist you with weekly check-ins and in depth breakdowns of the application processes as well as connecting you with potential mentors or experts. You are also free to continue with the free track if you prefer a more hands-on approach.
Here's a list of what we offer as a company for free users:
  1. Resume reviews
  2. Tips on how to apply for jobs
  3. Sample questions for interviews
  4. Calendar to plan out process
  5. Storage of user information
  6. Resume building tips
  7. Jobs recommended to user based on attributes and location
  8. Planner with pre-generated deadline based on input
